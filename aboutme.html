About Me

My name is Hunter Saine. I'm an electrician for a regional heating and air company in North Georgia. I started my career as an electrical apprentice with my current company, and I worked my way up to Senior Electrician. After spending some time in this position, I decided to attend university with the goal of personal and professional development. I continued to work full-time while pursuing my education. Through a lot of hard work and determination, I was able to graduate from Kennesaw State University in 2022 with a B.S. in Political Science. I'm currently pursuing a second degree in Computer Science at the University of North Georgia. I love working with new technologies, and I enjoy the challenges of solving complex problems. I'm learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript right now. I plan on learning Python next.

When I'm not working, I love to travel. I have a passion for exploring different cultures, and I use my experiences abroad to help shape my understanding of the world. I believe that we should learn from the experiences of others, as well as our own, to develop and implement policies that would benefit us all.
