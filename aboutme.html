My name is Hunter Saine. I'm an installation technician for AT&T. I started my career as an electrical apprentice with a regional heating and air company in North Georgia, and I worked my way up to Senior Electrician. After spending some time in this position, I decided to attend university with the goal of personal and professional development. I continued to work full-time while pursuing my education. Through a lot of hard work and determination, I was able to graduate from Kennesaw State University in 2022 with a B.S. in Political Science. After working for the same company for nine years, I decided it was time to search for new oppurtunities. I was excited when AT&T offered me a position. I'm currently pursuing a second degree in Computer Science at Oregon State University. I love working with new technologies, and I enjoy the challenges of solving complex problems. I'm learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript right now. I plan on learning Python next. When I'm not working, I love to travel. I have a passion for exploring different cultures, and I use my experiences abroad to help shape my understanding of the world. I believe that we should learn from the experiences of others, as well as our own, to develop and implement policies that would benefit us all. If you agree and would like to work together, contact me. I look forward to hearing from you.
